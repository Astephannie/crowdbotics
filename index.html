Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

Crowdbotics deploys vetted, high-quality dev resources straight into Slack, Asana, and Github.

Where do the people come from?

All over the world, but especially Latin America, the Middle East, and rural America.

What languages and frameworks are supported?

CSS, HTML, Django, Python, React, and Javascript.

Why is it so low-cost?

It's all being subsidized by a machine learning operation. We're studying developer chat interactions to train a neural network to generate code automatically (AI for code). In the meantime, you get inexpensive developer resources.
